About Agustin

Agustin Zavala (@Biotech_Bros) is a healthcare consultant for Bionest Partners. He's the host of the Biotech Bros podcast and managing director of Biosphere Capital Management.

Agustin graduated from Princeton University, with a degree in molecular biology and is also a jiu-jitsu practitioner and all round awesome person.

Episode Contents

In the episode, Avthar and Agustin discuss:

  • Agustin's creator's journey and why he started a biotech investing podcast and investment fund

  • How he's discovered his natural strengths and interests

  • How Agustin listens to his audience and acts based on their feedback

  • Why being consistent and taking small steps is your best bet to success

  • How content killed the resume and the value of being a content creator

  • How to find business or project ideas

  • The Future of content, side hustles and monetization

Clip 1: Being Consistent and Taking small steps

Q: How can you ensure success in a new business, project or creative pursuit?

A: Build momentum by being consistent and taking small steps toward your goal

In this clip from LWA #2, Avthar and Agustin discuss how their past mistakes have taught them the power of consistency and taking actionable steps, as well as why it's important to be in the "Clouds" and "Dirt" simultaneously as a creator and founder.

Clip 4: The Creator's Journey: Biotech podcasting and investing

Agustin Zavala (Biotech_Bros) shares his journey to becoming a creator and entrepreneur.
