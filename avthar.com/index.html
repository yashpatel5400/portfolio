How to Find Your Strengths — Awareness of your strengths helps you maximize your impact. The Founder Mentality — I took the leap to start a company as my first ‘real job’ straight out of college. The experience was exciting, scary and, at times, exhausting, but it equipped me with the most important mindset for success in business and in my career: The Founder Mentality.

Don’t Outsource Your Thinking — This is the biggest mistake I see young founders make. I even made it myself as a first time founder. Avoid this at all costs.

Startups and Self Awareness — Self Awareness is knowing who you actually are, what you’re good at and being at peace with it. It’s the key for budding entrepreneurs to create wealth and win.

How to Program Your Mind for Fitness — If getting unhealthy is effortless, can getting healthy be effortless as well? The answer is yes, but the secret isn’t an exercise or a diet. 🙏 Happiness

Self Worth and Mental Health — Conditional self-worth is a contract you make with yourself to be unhappy about being yourself until conditions outside your control are met.

Focus on What You Can Control — Focus on what you can control. Accept everything else. This and 6 other takeaways from The Manual by Epictetus (aka Enchiridion). It’s a book that transformed the way I think about living a successful and peaceful life
